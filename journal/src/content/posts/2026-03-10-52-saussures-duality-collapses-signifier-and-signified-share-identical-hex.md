## Interpretation

The signifier/signified collapse reveals a genuine trait-set boundary. UHT traits describe *what kind of thing* something is — both signifier and signified are symbolic, rule-governed, compositional social constructs that process signals at a meta level. They differ in *role within a relation* (form vs. content), and UHT has no trait for relational role. This is not a misclassification: it is an accurate statement that signifier and signified are the same *kind* of entity, differentiated only by their position in a dyadic structure.

The 0000E080 attractor (denotation/icon/index) captures "passive signs" — entities that carry meaning without processing or analyzing it. Stripping away Processes Signals/Logic and Meta from the semiotics profile yields exactly this pattern.

The semiotics-mathematics proximity (Jaccard 0.889) confirms a pattern from prior sessions: disciplines that study abstract formal structures converge in UHT regardless of subject matter. Mathematics studies quantity/structure; semiotics studies signs/meaning. Both are synthetic, rule-governed, compositional, symbolic meta-frameworks.
