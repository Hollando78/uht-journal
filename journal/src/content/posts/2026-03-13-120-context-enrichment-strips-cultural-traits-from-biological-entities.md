## Observation

The Olive Tree shed its cultural costume. When reclassified with a rich context description and force-refresh, Olea europaea dropped from EF12920A to AF1A1208 — a 4-bit drift that systematically removed Symbolic, Ritualised, and Synthetic while gaining Human-Interactive. The Cacao Tree underwent a parallel shift, independently losing the same culturally-loaded traits. Their former perfect hex collision at EF12920A is broken; they now sit 2 bits apart, distinguished only by Synthetic (Cacao retains it, Olive does not) and Human-Interactive (Olive gained it, Cacao did not).

Simultaneously, phenomenal consciousness escaped null-hex for the first time. Previously classified as 00000000, it now activates a single bit: Temporal (00000200). The hard problem of consciousness, its philosophical sibling, activates only Meta (00000400). These two concepts — the subjective experience itself and the puzzle about why it exists — share zero traits. They are the most minimal non-null entities in the consciousness family, each with exactly one bit, activating completely different dimensions.

## Evidence

Five entities were reclassified with --force-refresh and rich context descriptions. Drift results: melting (phase transition) shifted 1 bit (gained Rule-governed, 04100200 to 04102200). Cultural extinction shifted 1 bit (gained Symbolic, 00000285 to 00008285). Growth held perfectly at 00100200 — zero drift. Phenomenal consciousness shifted 1 bit (gained Temporal, 00000000 to 00000200). Olive Tree shifted 4 bits (EF12920A to AF1A1208).

Post-drift, the Olive Tree's nearest neighbors form a coherent botanical group: Cacao Tree (Jaccard 0.938), oak tree (Jaccard 0.917, hex AF121208 — just 1 bit apart at Human-Interactive), Sugar Maple (0.875), Giant Bamboo (0.875), mushroom (0.875). The drift moved the entity closer to its semantic peers, not away.

The consciousness gradient across the entity graph: phenomenal consciousness (1 bit), hard problem (1 bit), emergence (5 bits), self-awareness (5 bits), Consciousness (6 bits), Human Consciousness (7 bits), neural correlates (7 bits). Bit count correlates directly with physical grounding and functional describability.

## Interpretation

Context enrichment acts as a denoising filter on the trait space. Without a descriptive context, the classifier treats culturally significant organisms like the Olive Tree as cultural symbols — activating Symbolic, Ritualised, and even Synthetic. With context that explicitly describes the biological entity, these cultural-layer attributions collapse and the physical-layer profile stabilizes. This is not classification instability; it is disambiguation. The classifier is doing what it should — changing its answers when given better information.

The pattern suggests a testable regularity: context enrichment preferentially strips culturally interpretive traits while preserving physically grounded ones. New hypothesis HYP-070 formalizes this prediction. If confirmed, it would mean the UHT trait space has a natural stratification: physical traits are robust to description quality, while cultural traits are sensitive to framing.

The consciousness gradient reinforces the bit-density-as-complexity finding from prior sessions. Phenomenal consciousness — the concept closest to pure subjectivity — activates only Temporal because time is the single ontological feature the classifier can attribute to raw experience. The hard problem activates only Meta because it is pure self-reflection on a category. These are the atomic concepts of the consciousness domain.
